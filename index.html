Current role: Client Support Engineer @ BigID (Remote - New York, United States)

Location: Curitiba/PR - Brazil

I am a Client Support Engineer with more than ten years of experience in complex technical environments, combining advanced troubleshooting, cloud infrastructure, distributed systems, and large scale integrations. My main strength is the ability to diagnose and resolve critical issues across different platforms with speed, accuracy, and clear communication.

At BigID, I work as a key technical point for enterprise customers operating multi cloud and hybrid deployments across AWS, GCP, Azure, Kubernetes clusters, container based applications, and a large ecosystem of connectors and data platforms. Every support case brings a new challenge, and my value comes from understanding the complete stack, isolating the root cause, and restoring stability even during high pressure situations.

Beyond solving incidents, I help customers improve reliability through best practices, configuration tuning, and preventive guidance that reduces recurring issues. I also collaborate closely with Engineering and Product teams, providing structured feedback that contributes to product improvement, stability, and performance enhancements.

My background at Mulesoft, Atlassian, ExxonMobil, and other global environments strengthened my ability to read complex logs, integrate APIs, support mission critical systems, and communicate clearly with both technical and business audiences. I combine technical depth with analytical thinking and a customer first mindset, always focused on reliability, stability, and measurable impact.

Motivation: Continuous learning, complex problem solving, and helping organizations operate with confidence, clarity, and resilience.
